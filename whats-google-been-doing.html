What's Google been doing?

Jim Muttram May 8, 2009

It's Sergey Brin's turn to write the annual Google Founders Letter, a traditional part of Google's annual report. If you have some spare time it makes a great read, reflecting on the past decade and looking forward to some of the developments which Google believes will come in the next 10 years. These include AI, automatic machine translation and embedded search. Well worth a read.
