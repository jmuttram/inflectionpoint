Skype futures

Jim Muttram March 20, 2007

Niklas Zennstrom of Skype is quoted by Jeff Jarvis talking about the future. He has some interesting thoughts on what comes next: Skypefind enabling users in 200 countries to find - and rate - businesses (enabling a business model based on advertising and pay-per-lead); enabling user to user commerce using PayPal to make the payment; and enabling users to send money to each other. Starts to look like eBay made a smart purchase...
