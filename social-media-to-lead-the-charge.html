Social media to lead the charge?

Jim Muttram May 3, 2008

Social media should benefit the most during a recession, according to research from Forrester. Josh Bernoff blogged the results which seem to show that a larger proportion of interactive marketers plan to increase spend in Social Networking, User generated content, email marketing and blogging than other categories.
