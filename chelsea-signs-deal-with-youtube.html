Chelsea signs deal with YouTube

Jim Muttram February 22, 2007

Chelsea FC has struck a deal to show archive and daily news footage on a Chelsea branded page at www.youtube.com/chelseafc, reports the Guardian. Broadcasting rights deal prevent live coverage. The content is produced by a JV between Chelsea and BskyB.
